Core Ethical Principles

  • Ethical Frameworks: Explore the three frameworks - Consequentialism, Deontology, and Virtue Ethics.
  • Informed Consent: Understand the importance of transparency and voluntary agreement.
  • Data Security: Learn best practices for safeguarding sensitive information.
  • Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and their consequences.
  • Integrity: Commit to honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Adaptability: Be flexible and responsive to ethical challenges.
